Coding Journey

How it started?



Three years ago, I started coding when I was in Grade 12 at La Consolacion University Philippines. We had a programming subject where we used the C# language and skipped the c/c++ though I found it really helpful. Of course, just like the other students, I had difficulty understanding the codes. I remember during our first activity, I could not finish it because I had no idea how to print the sum.


During my first year at Bulacan State University, we started learning about the c++. It was easy for me since I already have some knowledge about programming from my previous year. I was up-to-date with the lessons and able to answer the activities.

On my second year, we studied about JAVA language. And since they introduced the c# language to us initially, i did not struggle adopting the new language since JAVA and C# is similar.

At the moment, I am learning about HTML and Css which is quite challenging. Although, I do not have any background about “markup text language” yet, I am capable of creating any basic webpages through self study and by watching YouTube videos.

My First Game



One time, I saw my nephew playing Roblox and said “Is that it? Boring.”

I tried playing it once and unexpectedly enjoyed the game. I also found out that Roblox is like a game sharing community where heaps of people develop games and earn money from it.

An idea came to my mind … "what if I learn how to create a game on roblox?” So, I started researching, watching on YouTube, and reading articles about it.



On the 12th of October 2021, at 6:52:03 AM 10/12/2021, I released my first game, an obby game.
